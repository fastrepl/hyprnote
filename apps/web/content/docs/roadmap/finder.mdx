Notetaking is an intense activity. Your information and knowledge get spread around everywhere, harder to find what you need as more notes are created.

No matter if it's an AI or not, the essence of notetaker is a great notetaking experience, and we care deeploy about that. That is why we created the Finder view, a one place for viewing all your notes and information stored in Hyprnote.

You can see the Finter view by clicking the folder icon next to the settings button. There are 3 currently available modes in the finder view. Let's go over them one by one.
