People who believe they deserve better than vendor lock-in and subscription treadmills. They want powerful AI tools on their terms.

- **Autonomy-first** — Choose your own AI models, on-device or cloud
- **Data-conscious** — Their notes stay theirs, not training data
- **Tech-forward** — Excited about local AI, want tools that harness it
- **Open source advocates** — Value transparency and extensibility
