## Meeting Context

Investor name, firm, stage focus, and context for the meeting.

## Pitch Summary

Key points presented about the problem, solution, market, traction, and ask.

## Questions Asked

All questions the investor asked about the business, market, team, or metrics.

## Feedback Received

Investor's reactions, concerns, enthusiasm, and specific feedback on the pitch.

## Investment Interest

Investor's level of interest, potential fit, and likelihood of investment.

## Follow-up Items

Information or materials requested, additional meetings scheduled, and next steps.
