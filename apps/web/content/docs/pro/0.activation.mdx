---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Buy Pro License">
    You'll receive a confirmation email with your license key.
    <Image
      src="/api/images/docs/pro/activation-1.png"
      alt="Hyprnote Activation Step 1"
      title="Hyprnote Activation Step 1"
    />
    The license key will look like this:

    ```bash
    key/******************************************
    ```

  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to settings">
    Open Hyprnote and navigate to Settings. Paste the license key into the text input.
    <Image
      src="/api/images/docs/pro/activation-2.png"
      alt="Hyprnote Activation Step 2"
      title="Hyprnote Activation Step 2"
    />
  </Step>
</Steps>
