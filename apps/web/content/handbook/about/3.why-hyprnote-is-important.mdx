Most AI tools force you into their ecosystem, their models, their rules. You're locked into subscriptions, dependent on cloud services, and have no real control over your data. Hyprnote proves another path is possible:

## Our Differentiators

**Model flexibility.** Use on-device AI, bring your own models, or mix and match. Your choice.

**Data ownership.** Your data stays yours. Local-first, privacy-first.

**Open source.** Transparent, extensible, and community-driven.

**Future-proof.** Hardware is advancing rapidly, and local AI is becoming the norm. Hyprnote is built to harness this shift.

## Our Belief

We believe everyone deserves software that works for them, not against them. You shouldn't need enterprise budgets to have powerful, flexible AI tools that respect your autonomy.
