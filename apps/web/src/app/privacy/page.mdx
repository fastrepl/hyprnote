# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 16, 2024

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Hyprnote ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our desktop application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Audio Data

- System audio recordings
- Microphone recordings
- Voice transcriptions

### 2.2 User Content

- Notes and annotations
- AI-augmented content
- User preferences and settings

### 2.3 Technical Data

- System permissions status
- Application performance data
- Error logs

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Provide voice transcription services
- Generate AI-augmented notes
- Improve our services
- Troubleshoot technical issues

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Local Storage

- All audio recordings are stored locally on your device
- Transcriptions are processed and stored locally
- Notes and augmentations are saved on your device

### 4.2 Security Measures

- End-to-end encryption for data processing
- Secure local storage
- System-level permission controls

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties except:

- When required by law
- With your explicit consent
- For processing necessary to provide our services

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access your data
- Delete your data
- Export your data
- Modify app permissions

## 7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We will notify users of any material changes to this policy.
