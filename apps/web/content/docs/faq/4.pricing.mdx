## Is Char free?

Char offers a free tier with core features. Pro features like unlimited recording time, advanced templates, and priority support are available through our paid plans.

## Can I try Pro features before subscribing?

Yes! New users get a 14-day free trial of Char Pro to try all features before committing to a subscription.
