Hyprnote is a privacy-first AI notepad for meetings. It's [open-source](https://github.com/fastrepl/hyprnote), runs [locally](/docs/about/why-local-first), and works without bots joining your calls.

Key features:
- Transcribe and summarize locally or with your own models
- No data leaves your device unless you choose cloud features
- Built with [Tauri](https://github.com/tauri-apps/tauri) for native performance
