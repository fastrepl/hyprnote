## Control Your Data

Self-hosting Hyprnote means you have complete control over your data. No more worrying about third-party servers or data leaks. Your notes, your rules.

## Enhanced Privacy

With self-hosting, you can keep your sensitive information away from prying eyes. It's the ultimate solution for privacy-conscious users.

## Customization

Self-hosting allows you to customize your Hyprnote experience. Tweak the settings, add features, and make it truly yours.
