We give people back their focus and their autonomy. Instead of frantically taking notes or being locked into a vendor's ecosystem, our users can be fully present in their conversations with complete control over their AI and data.

## Core Value Propositions

**Freedom of choice** — Use any AI model you want. On-device for privacy, cloud for power, or switch between them.

**True ownership** — Your data stays yours. No vendor lock-in, no forced cloud dependency.

**Time savings** — No more manual note-taking or post-meeting documentation.

**Better recall** — Searchable transcripts and summaries mean nothing gets lost.

**Simplicity** — Powerful features that feel effortless to use, without sacrificing control.
