## Free Trial

New users can try Hyprnote Pro free for 14 days. During onboarding, select "Start Free Trial" to begin. The trial includes full access to all Pro features including cloud transcription and AI services. When your trial begins, you'll see a congratulatory modal confirming your Pro access.

## Subscribing to Pro

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an Account">
    Open Hyprnote and sign in or create an account. You can also sign up at [hyprnote.com/pricing](/pricing).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a Plan">
    Visit [hyprnote.com/pricing](/pricing) to view available plans and subscribe. Your subscription will be linked to your account automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Using Pro">
    Once subscribed, Pro features are enabled immediately. Go to Settings > Account to verify your subscription status.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

If you're logged out while using Pro features, you'll see a toast notification prompting you to log back in. Your Pro status is preserved across app restarts, but you'll need an active internet connection to validate your subscription periodically.
