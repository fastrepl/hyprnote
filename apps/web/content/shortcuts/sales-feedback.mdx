## When to use

Use this shortcut after a sales discovery call, demo, or negotiation to get AI-powered feedback on your performance.

## What you'll get

- Analysis of your discovery questions and listening skills
- Evaluation of how you handled objections
- Feedback on your value proposition delivery
- Assessment of rapport building and relationship development
- Specific, actionable suggestions for improvement

## Example questions to ask

- "What could I have done better in this call?"
- "How well did I handle the pricing objection?"
- "Did I ask enough discovery questions?"
