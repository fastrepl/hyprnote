## Why Char?

Char captures your meetings, transcribes them locally, and generates summaries - all without bots joining your calls. It's designed to be simple: just open it and start your meeting.

## Our Vision

Char will remember everything that's said, derive insights, and act on your behalf. Your trusted companion for work.
