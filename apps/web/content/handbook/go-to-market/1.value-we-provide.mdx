## Bring Your Own Key

If you already have API credits from OpenAI, Anthropic, or other providers, why pay twice? Char lets you use your existing keys instead of forcing you onto another subscription. This is the single most resonant value prop for our audience.

- **Freedom of choice** — Any AI model, on-device or cloud, no lock-in
- **True ownership** — Files on your disk, local-first, never dependent on our servers
- **Time savings** — No manual note-taking or post-meeting docs
- **Better recall** — Searchable transcripts, nothing gets lost
- **Simplicity** — Powerful features that feel effortless
